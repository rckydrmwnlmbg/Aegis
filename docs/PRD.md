# 1. Executive Product Definition

## 1.1 Product Identity

Product Name:

**Aegis AI EHS Platform**

Canonical classification:

**Enterprise AI-Native Environmental, Health, and Safety Operating Platform**

Aegis is a unified enterprise-grade digital operating system designed to govern industrial Environmental, Health, and Safety execution, intelligence, compliance, and operational oversight.

Aegis is not a narrow incident reporting application.

Aegis is not a checklist application.

Aegis is not a digital document repository.

Aegis is not a compliance-only governance portal.

Aegis is a full-spectrum HSE operating platform.

---

## 1.2 Product Mission

Aegis exists to transform fragmented industrial HSE operational ecosystems into governed, intelligent, auditable, digitally orchestrated enterprise operations.

The platform shall support:

- field safety execution
- hazard governance
- incident intelligence
- permit governance
- structured operational risk planning
- corrective action governance
- contractor governance
- workforce readiness
- compliance governance
- audit readiness
- executive operational intelligence
- AI-assisted workflow augmentation

---

## 1.3 Product Scope Doctrine

Aegis shall maintain full-spectrum enterprise HSE scope.

Product scope is intentionally broad.

Scope shall not be artificially reduced at doctrine level.

Implementation architecture must support this scope without compromising architectural correctness.

---

## 1.4 Supported Delivery Models

The product must support:

- SaaS multi-tenant

- hybrid enterprise deployment

AI within Aegis exists as governed augmentation infrastructure.

AI is not workflow authority.

AI does not replace accountable enterprise governance.

---

## Product Role of AI

AI shall augment:

- structured operational reporting
- workflow drafting
- knowledge retrieval
- document navigation
- analytics interpretation
- summarization
- classification assistance
- recommendation support

---

## Explicit AI Non-Authority Rules

AI shall NOT:

- approve permits
- approve CAPA closure
- finalize incident severity autonomously
- assign disciplinary responsibility
- determine regulatory compliance conclusively
- authorize contractor participation
- override governance workflows
- make legal determinations
- perform autonomous incident adjudication
